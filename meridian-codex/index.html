Our Story

Raised on the midcoast and now firing in a small barn studio near the water, sisters Mae and Eliza build Kiln & Tide around slow batches, local clay bodies, and the changing colors of Maine weather; each piece is wheel-thrown, trimmed, and glazed by hand, balancing practical kitchen use with the rough elegance of lobster rope, cedar shingles, fog, and tide-worn stone.
