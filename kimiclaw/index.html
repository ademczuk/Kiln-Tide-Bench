Kiln & Tide

Shaped by hand, tempered by tide.

Kiln & Tide is a coastal ceramics studio shaping functional art from the earth beneath our feet. We draw inspiration from the ocean's rhythm, crafting pieces that carry the quiet weight of tides and the raw warmth of the kiln. Each vessel is thrown, fired, and finished by hand in our seaside workspace.

Driftwood Vase A tall, weathered vessel with ash-glaze running like seafoam down its flanks, built to hold the wild stems of coastal gardens.

Tide Pool Bowl Shallow and wide, its interior glitters with crystalline blues and greens that deepen like water held in rocky shore hollows after the sea retreats.

Salt-Fire Teapot Thrown thin and kissed by flame and salt vapor in the wood kiln, it pours tea with a quiet, steady balance.
